About Indian Cricket Academy

Indian Cricket Academy was established in 2016 with a clear vision—to shape the next generation of cricketing talent. Over the years, we've grown into a hub for aspiring cricketers, guided by passion and purpose.

Our academy offers training on matting, turf, and cement wickets, with dedicated spaces for fielding drills and net sessions. With advanced equipment and experienced coaching staff, Indian Cricket Academy is committed to nurturing skill, discipline, and sportsmanship in every young player.

Love cricket? Crazy about football?

At our Cricket and Football Academy, we believe every athlete begins with a spark — a passion for the game that, with the right guidance, can evolve into extraordinary skill and achievement. Established with the vision of creating a professional and nurturing environment for budding sports talent, our academy has grown into a trusted space where discipline meets enthusiasm, and training meets transformation.

We take pride in our team of highly experienced coaches and trainers who bring with them years of playing and coaching experience at state, national, and international levels. Their hands-on approach, deep game insight, and commitment to each player's progress ensure that students receive not just coaching, but mentorship.

Our infrastructure is built to international standards, including turf and mat wickets for cricket, full-sized football grounds, fitness and recovery zones, and technical training modules. More than just physical training, we also emphasize tactical awareness, teamwork, mental conditioning, and leadership skills—qualities essential for success both on the field and beyond.

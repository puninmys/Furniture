About T3 Infra

Creating Iconic Structures with a Commitment to Quality

At T3 Infra, our 20+ years in the field allow us to develop unique properties that reflect the trust and admiration of our valued customers. We believe in turning your vision of a dream home into reality.

Nestled in prime locations across South and North Bangalore, we offer a perfect blend of serenity and convenience for all our customers. Whether you are looking for a residential plot or a peaceful farmland retreat, T3 INFRA is your ultimate destination.

Founded by Prakash, who brings over 20 years of experience in land and property development, T3 INFRA stands tall as a trusted name in the real estate industry. Under his guidance, we have successfully developed numerous properties, earning a reputation for quality and reliability. Our services include converting lands into DC-converted sites with approvals from major authorities such as BBMP, BDA, and BIAPPA, ensuring that your investment is legally secure and future-proof. Whether it's residential plots or farmlands, every project is executed with precision and integrity.

At T3INFRA, we don’t just develop land; we create communities designed for quality living. Our well-planned layouts and serene surroundings make for the perfect setting to build your future. Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat away from the city or a strategically located plot for your dream home, T3INFRA offers it all. From DC-converted plots to BBMP-approved and BDA NOC sites, our developments cater to diverse needs, ensuring hassle-free ownership and an elevated lifestyle.
